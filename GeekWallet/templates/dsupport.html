Support

How do I record a new transaction?

+

Click the “+ Record Transaction” button from the dashboard or navigation bar. Then enter the transaction details such as amount, category, date, and notes before clicking “Save.”

How do I create a budget?

+

Navigate to the Budget Management page and click “Create New Budget.” Select a category, enter the budget amount, choose the budget period, and save the budget plan.

How do recurring transactions work?

+

When recurring mode is enabled during transaction creation, the system automatically repeats the transaction based on the selected payment cycle such as daily, weekly, monthly, or yearly.

Can I edit or delete existing transactions?

+

Yes. Users can open the Records page to view transaction history, edit transaction details, or permanently delete transactions when necessary.

Why is my dashboard showing no data?

+

The dashboard will remain empty until transactions, budgets, or saving goals are added. New users should begin by recording their first transaction.

How do I change my password?

+

Go to the Settings page and select “Change Password.” Enter the current password, create a new password, confirm it, and click “Save.”

Can I customize transaction categories?

+

Yes. Users can create, edit, and delete income or expense categories from the Categories Management page.

Why can’t I delete a category?

+

Categories connected to existing transactions cannot be deleted directly because transaction history depends on those categories. Users may need to move transactions to another category first.

What happens if I exceed my budget?

+

When total expenses exceed the budget limit, the system will display a budget exceeded warning message. This helps users recognize overspending early and manage expenses more carefully.
