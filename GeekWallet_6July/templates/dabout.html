GeekWallet

We are a team of eleven students from the Diploma of Computer Science (Batch 2) at Spring University Myanmar . This system was developed as our final project through collaboration, learning, creativity, and strong team spirit. Throughout this journey, we supported one another, shared ideas, solved challenges together, and turned our vision into reality.

Our goal is to create a simple and accessible financial management system that helps users build healthier spending, budgeting, and saving habits in their daily lives. Building this project was not easy, but our friendship, dedication, and teamwork helped us move forward together as one team with one dream. This project reflects not only our technical learning journey but also the trust, communication, and collaboration we developed throughout the process. We carefully designed this system with a strong focus on usability, simplicity, and user-friendly experience so that users can manage their personal finances more easily and confidently.

As students, this project gave us valuable real-world experience in system design, problem-solving, teamwork, and software development. It allowed us to grow together while applying the knowledge and skills we learned during our studies. We hope this project can encourage better financial habits and become a helpful expense tracking solution for the Myanmar community in the future.
