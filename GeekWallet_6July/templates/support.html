Frequently Asked Questions

What is Geek Wallet? +

Geek Wallet is designed to help users manage personal finances more effectively by tracking income, expenses, and budget goals in one place. It helps users understand spending habits, monitor financial activity, and build healthier financial management habits.

Why do I need to create an account? +

Creating an account allows the user to securely store and manage personal financial records. It also enables users to access their data anytime and continue tracking their financial progress across sessions.

Can I record both income and expenses? +

Yes. Users can record both income and expense transactions. Tracking both types of transactions helps users monitor their balance, understand cash flow, and manage finances more accurately.

Can I edit or delete transactions after saving them? +

Yes. Transactions can be edited or deleted anytime through the Records page. This allows users to correct mistakes, update information, or remove unnecessary records from their transaction history.

What are recurring transactions? +

Recurring transactions are transactions that repeat automatically based on a selected schedule, such as monthly bills, subscriptions, salaries, or regular payments. This feature helps users avoid entering the same transactions repeatedly.
